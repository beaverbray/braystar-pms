## Overview

Conduct comprehensive competitive analysis of property management software vendors, focusing on collections features, pricing, positioning, and AI capabilities. Identify competitive advantages and develop strategies to win against incumbents.

## Strategic Context

- **Direct Competitors:** AppFolio, Buildium (RealPage), Yardi Breeze, DoorLoop, TenantCloud
- **Indirect Competitors:** Knock CRM, ServiceTitan, Property Meld
- **Key Threats:** AppFolio Realm-X AI, RealPage consolidation, DoorLoop AI features
- **Our Advantage:** Sidecar approach, compliance-by-design, collections depth

5. **Monitor competitive threats** with ongoing intelligence system

## Key Research Questions

### Landscape Mapping
1. Who are all the property management software vendors in the market?
2. How do they segment by property type, size, and functionality?
3. Who focuses on collections/payments specifically?
4. What adjacent categories could move into our space?

### Feature Analysis
5. What collections features do incumbents offer?
6. How sophisticated is their automation (basic reminders vs. behavioral intelligence)?
7. What compliance features do they provide (TCPA, FDCPA, Fair Housing)?
8. What AI/ML capabilities have they launched or announced?
9. What integrations do they offer (payment processors, accounting, etc.)?

### Pricing Analysis
10. What are the pricing models (per-unit, flat fee, tiered, etc.)?
11. What are typical minimums and contract terms?
12. What's included vs. add-on pricing?
13. How do they position value vs. cost?

### Go-to-Market Analysis
14. How do they position themselves (messaging, taglines, value props)?
15. What channels do they use for customer acquisition?
16. What is their sales process (self-service, inside sales, enterprise)?
17. How do they onboard customers (migration process, timeline, support)?

### Strengths & Weaknesses
18. What do customers love about each competitor (review analysis)?
19. What do customers complain about (pain points, churn reasons)?
20. What are their technical limitations or legacy constraints?
21. Where are they vulnerable to disruption?

### 3. Competitive Feature Matrix
- **Format:** Spreadsheet with scoring
- **Features to Compare:**
  - Collections automation (basic vs. behavioral)
  - Communication channels (SMS, email, voice, portal)
  - Compliance (TCPA, FDCPA, Fair Housing)
  - Payment processing (ACH, card, payment plans)
  - Analytics and reporting
  - Resident self-service
  - AI/ML capabilities
  - Integration options (PMS, accounting, banks)
  - Mobile app vs. web-only
  - Trust accounting
  - Maintenance coordination
  - Lease management
- **Scoring:** Yes/No/Limited for each feature
- **Analysis:** Feature gap analysis, our differentiation

### 4. Competitive Pricing Comparison
- **Format:** Table with normalized pricing
- **Contents:**
  - Pricing model by competitor
  - Cost per unit for standard tiers
  - Minimums and contract terms
  - Total cost of ownership (TCO) for 100, 200, 500 units
  - Our pricing positioning (competitive, premium, or value)

### 5. Win/Loss Analysis Framework
- **Format:** Template for sales team
- **Contents:**
  - Why we win (key differentiators validated by wins)
  - Why we lose (competitive weaknesses to address)
  - Common objections by competitor
  - Proof points and case studies to counter
  - Battle cards (one-pager per key competitor)

### 6. Competitive Intelligence Tracking System
- **Format:** Living document/database
- **Contents:**
  - Competitor product release notes
  - Pricing changes and promotions
  - Executive changes and strategy shifts
  - Customer wins/losses
  - Review site rating trends
  - Funding and M&A activity
- **Update Cadence:** Monthly

- **Customer Interviews:** "What other tools did you evaluate?" (discovery calls)

- **Public Filings:** AppFolio 10-K, investor presentations, earnings calls
