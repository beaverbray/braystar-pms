## Overview

Execute focused 6-week validation plan to prove that: (1) collections pain is acute enough to drive buying behavior, (2) 30-day pilot can demonstrate measurable ROI, (3) pricing is acceptable for target segment, and (4) product concept resonates with decision-makers.

## Strategic Context

- **Validation Philosophy:** Learn fast, fail fast, pivot if needed
- **Investment:** <$10K in costs (time is main investment)
- **Decision Gate:** If ‚â•2 of 3 pilots convert to paid ‚Üí proceed to launch
- **Output:** Customer insights, validated messaging, proven ROI, 2-3 paying customers

- [ ] Execute 30-day pilots with 3 customers

- [ ] Zero TCPA/Fair Housing complaints

### Interview Question Framework

**Setup (2 minutes):**
> "Thanks for taking the time. I'm building a tool to help property managers reduce late rent without burning out staff or pestering tenants. Before I go too far, I want to make sure I'm solving a real problem. I have about 20 questions‚Äîshould take 20-30 minutes. Sound good?"

#### Problem Discovery (10 minutes)

1. **Walk me through what happens when rent is late on the 6th of the month.** Who does what?
   - *Goal: Understand current manual process*

2. **What % of your tenants pay on time (1st-5th)? What % are late but eventually pay? What % go to eviction?**
   - *Goal: Quantify on-time rate baseline*

3. **How many hours per week does your team spend on collections follow-up (calls, texts, emails)?**
   - *Goal: Quantify staff time burden*

4. **What tools/systems do you currently use for collections reminders?**
   - *Goal: Understand current tech stack*

#### Pain Quantification (5 minutes)

5. **What's your current Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) for rent collection?**
   - *Goal: Measure cash flow impact*

6. **What does late rent cost you?** (Staff time, bad debt write-offs, cash flow stress)
   - *Goal: Quantify total cost of problem*

7. **If you could wave a magic wand and improve on-time rent by 15%, what would that be worth to you annually?**
   - *Goal: Validate ROI framing*

#### Solution Validation (7 minutes)

8. **What would make you try a new collections tool without switching your entire PMS?**
   - *Goal: Validate sidecar approach*

9. **What risks or concerns would stop you from piloting something like this?**
   - *Goal: Identify objections*

10. **If we could prove 15% improvement in 30 days, would you pay $[X]/month?** (Test pricing sensitivity)
    - *Goal: Validate pricing*

#### Buying Process (3 minutes)

11. **Who else would need to be involved in this decision?** (Owner, CFO, compliance officer?)
    - *Goal: Identify economic buyer and influencers*

12. **What's your typical process for evaluating new software?**
    - *Goal: Understand sales cycle*

13. **Have you tried other collections tools? Why did or didn't they work?**
    - *Goal: Learn from competitive experiences*

#### Compliance & Risk (2 minutes)

14. **Have you ever dealt with TCPA or Fair Housing complaints related to collections?**
    - *Goal: Validate compliance concern*

15. **What keeps you up at night about compliance in rent collection?**
    - *Goal: Understand fear/risk perception*

#### Expansion Potential (2 minutes)

16. **After collections, what's your next biggest operational pain point?**
    - *Goal: Identify Phase 2 opportunities*

17. **Would you pay more for maintenance coordination if it integrated with collections?**
    - *Goal: Validate land-and-expand hypothesis*

#### Close & Pilot Recruitment (3 minutes)

18. **Based on what we've discussed, would you be interested in a 30-day pilot with 1-2 of your properties?**
    - *Goal: Gauge pilot interest*

**Closing:**
> "This has been super helpful. Last question: based on what we discussed, if I could prove a 15% improvement in on-time rent within 30 days with a no-cost pilot, would you be interested in testing it with 1-2 properties? No obligation, just a test."

### MVP Build (Week 3-4)

**Scope: Absolute Minimum for Pilot Success**

#### 1. CSV Import
- Support AppFolio, Yardi, Buildium export formats
- Parse tenant name, unit, phone, email, balance due, days late
- Validation and error handling
- Import history log

#### 2. Plaid Integration
- OAuth flow for bank account connection
- Detect incoming payments (transaction matching)
- Daily sync schedule
- Payment notification to update ledger

#### 3. Twilio Integration
- Send SMS with personalization tokens
- STOP keyword handling (instant opt-out)
- Delivery status tracking
- Message audit log

#### 4. SendGrid Integration
- Send email with personalization tokens
- Unsubscribe link handling
- Delivery status tracking
- Message audit log

#### 5. Simple Dashboard
- **KPIs:** On-time rate (baseline vs current), messages sent, response rate
- **Charts:** Funnel visualization (due ‚Üí reminded ‚Üí paid ‚Üí delinquent)
- **Table:** List of tenants with status (current, late, escalated)
- **Export:** CSV download of results

#### 6. Playbook Configurator
- Pre-built templates (friendly, neutral, urgent)
- Manager selects sequence for their properties
- Personalization tokens ([Tenant name], [balance], [due date])
- Schedule configuration (when to send each message)

**What We DON'T Build Yet (Phase 2):**
- ‚ùå AI/ML optimization (use pre-built playbooks only)

- **Hosting:** Heroku or Railway (simplest path to deploy)

## Post-Validation: Decision Point

### If Validation Succeeds (‚â•2 pilots convert to paid):

**‚úÖ Proceed to Launch**

### If Validation Fails (<2 pilots convert):

**üîç Deep-Dive Analysis**
- Why didn't pilots convert? (pricing, product, results, trust?)
- What would it take to convert them?
- Is the wedge right (collections) or should we pivot (maintenance)?

**Options:**
1. **Iterate & Re-Test:** Fix identified issues, run 3 more pilots
2. **Pivot Wedge:** Try maintenance as entry point instead of collections
3. **Pivot Segment:** Target different customer segment (e.g., SFR, student housing)
4. **Abandon:** Insufficient market pull, move to different opportunity

## Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Can't Recruit 3 Pilot Customers
- **Mitigation:** Start recruiting in Week 1, leverage warm network first, offer more value (longer pilot, free months)

### Risk 2: MVP Build Takes Longer Than 2 Weeks
- **Mitigation:** Reduce scope ruthlessly, use no-code tools (Retool, Airtable) if needed, hire contract dev

### Risk 3: Pilots Don't Show Measurable Results
- **Mitigation:** Set realistic expectations, choose properties with higher late rates (more room for improvement)

### Risk 4: Compliance Issues During Pilot
- **Mitigation:** Legal review of all templates, TCPA consent workflow, conservative messaging
